🚀 DRDO Fun Facts 🧠

🧠 Fun Fact 1
DRDO was born in 1958 with just 10 labs — now it powers over 50 cutting-edge research hubs across India! 🇮🇳
🛰️ Fun Fact 2
DRDO built the Agni and Prithvi missiles — India’s pride in strategic deterrence.
🛩️ Fun Fact 3
The Tejas jet? That’s DRDO’s gift to India — light, fast, and completely indigenous!
🚁 Fun Fact 4
DRDO invented bulletproof jackets like Bhabha Kavach, saving countless soldiers’ lives.
⚛️ Fun Fact 5
During COVID-19, DRDO quickly developed DIPAS ventilators and PPE kits. Lifesavers!
🧪 Fun Fact 6
The Akash missile system, capable of hitting targets up to 30 km, is DRDO’s innovation.
🧥 Fun Fact 7
DRDO helped India conduct the Pokhran nuclear tests — a historic moment in 1998.
🚀 Fun Fact 8
India’s anti-satellite (ASAT) missile mission in 2019? DRDO nailed it with Mission Shakti.
🌊 Fun Fact 9
DRDO isn’t all weapons — it also makes nutritious combat ration for soldiers at borders.
🧬 Fun Fact 10
DRDO developed sonars and torpedoes to protect India’s naval forces underwater.
🧠 Fun Fact 11
DRDO designed the Arjun Tank, a beast on tracks with modern firepower.
🛰️ Fun Fact 12
DRDO runs the Dare to Dream contest to fund startups and young inventors! 💡
🛡️ Fun Fact 13
Deployed in Siachen? Thank DRDO for those extreme cold climate suits and food packs.
🧪 Fun Fact 14
DRDO’s LRDE lab builds powerful radars that guard our skies 24/7.
💣 Fun Fact 15
DRDO created directional listening devices used by our forces during rescue ops.
🧫 Fun Fact 16
DRDO works on robotics and AI, helping automate surveillance and combat support.
🧊 Fun Fact 17
DRDO's Nishant drone does reconnaissance missions without risking human lives.
📡 Fun Fact 18
DRDO labs study stress management and psychology to support troop mental health.
🛰️ Fun Fact 19

🇮🇳 Fun Fact 20
From missiles to medicine, DRDO is India’s silent force in building a safer tomorrow. 🙌
