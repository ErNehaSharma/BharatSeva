🇮🇳 Central Government Fun Facts 🧐

🌕 Fun Fact 1
ISRO was founded in 1969, with its first satellite Aryabhata launched in 1975.
🚀 Fun Fact 2
India launched 104 satellites in one go in 2017 — a world record!
👨‍🔬 Fun Fact 3
ISRO's first rocket was transported on a bicycle and launched from a church!
🌍 Fun Fact 4
The Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan) made India the first Asian nation to reach Mars orbit.
💰 Fun Fact 5
Mangalyaan cost less than the Hollywood movie Gravity — talk about efficiency!
📡 Fun Fact 6
ISRO offers satellite services to over 45 countries around the globe.
🛰️ Fun Fact 7
The Gaganyaan mission will send Indian astronauts into space for the first time.
📶 Fun Fact 8
India has its own satellite navigation system called NavIC.
📷 Fun Fact 9
The Cartosat series provides high-resolution earth images used in agriculture, mapping, and defense.
🔍 Fun Fact 10
Chandrayaan-1 helped discover water on the Moon!
🌖 Fun Fact 11
Chandrayaan-3 achieved a successful soft landing on the Moon's south pole in 2023.
🚀 Fun Fact 12
ISRO's PSLV is known as the most reliable and versatile launch vehicle.
📡 Fun Fact 13
ISRO’s INSAT series supports telecom, broadcasting, and weather forecasting.
📡 Fun Fact 14
ISRO's RISAT satellites are used for all-weather and day-night observation.
🛰️ Fun Fact 15
ISRO supports disaster management by monitoring floods, cyclones, and droughts.
🏆 Fun Fact 16
ISRO received international praise for launching South Korea's and Israel’s satellites.
💻 Fun Fact 17
ISRO developed Bhuvan, a Google Earth-like mapping platform for India.
🛰️ Fun Fact 18
India's GSAT series enhances digital connectivity across rural regions.
🧪 Fun Fact 19
ISRO conducts experiments in microgravity using sounding rockets.
🛰️ Fun Fact 20
ISRO’s mission planning and budget control are studied by agencies like NASA!
