About DRDO

The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) is India's premier defense research and development agency, responsible for the development of cutting-edge technologies and systems for India's armed forces. DRDO plays a key role in strengthening national security.

Why Choose DRDO?

Working at DRDO provides the opportunity to engage in high-tech research and development projects that directly contribute to the defense and security of India. DRDO employees enjoy numerous benefits such as job security, research exposure, and an opportunity to serve the nation.
